From Roots to Refreshment, Our Beverage Journey

What began as a humble dream in our parents' small kitchen has grown into a vibrant legacy built on taste, tradition, and innovation.

Years ago, our parents, driven by a passion for authentic flavors and natural ingredients, started a small-scale beverage business. With simple tools, time-honored recipes, and a deep love for Indian taste traditions, they handcrafted syrups and concentrates that quickly became local favorites.

Their signature blends, especially the zesty Nimbu Ginger, tangy Lemon Masala, and tropical Pink Peru, captured the hearts of families looking for refreshing and nostalgic flavors.

Now, as the next generation—a brother-sister duo with unwavering dedication, they were joined by a few close, precious friends as extended family—who believed in the vision. Together, they poured passion, trust, and innovation into every bottle, giving birth to a renewed brand with a modern heartbeat and traditional soul.—we are proud to carry the torch forward. Inspired by our parents’ dedication and guided by modern innovation, we are taking the brand to new heights.

We've introduced a line of Ready-to-Serve (RTS) beverages crafted from the same treasured recipes, perfect for today’s fast-paced lifestyle without compromising on purity or taste. Alongside, our range of concentrates continues to empower homes, cafes, and businesses to serve quality drinks with ease and authenticity.
